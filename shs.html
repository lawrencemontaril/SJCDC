Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics

PRE-REGISTER

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) is an educational program developed to prepare primary and secondary students for college, graduate study and careers in the fields covered by STEM strand. In addition to subject-specific learning, STEM aims to foster inquiring minds, logical reasoning and collaboration skills.

The Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) strand seeks to prepare the young business leaders of tomorrow. ABM strand paves the way for business-related college degrees. It teaches the basic concepts of financial management, accounting, and corporate operations.

Humanities and Social Sciences

PRE-REGISTER

The HUMSS Senior High School strand is designed for students who intend to take up journalism, communication arts, liberal arts, education, and other social-science related courses in college.

General Academic Strand is for learners who are not yet decided on what course to take in college. It is possible that the learner will be given bridging courses to assess his/her preparedness for the field of study he/she will choose in the future. General Academic Strand will present the option for students to take their electives from specialized subjects of any other strands.

TVL (Food and Beverages NC II)

PRE-REGISTER

Food and Beverage Services NC II is a technical-vocational program that trains students on the preparation of food plans and meals for restaurants, hotels, canteens, banquets and functions, and basically any establishment that serves food to a large number of people. The program teaches students how to properly welcome guests and take food and beverage orders, promote food and beverage products and provide food and beverage service to guests.

Home Economics (Cookery) is responsive to the ever-changing market of food, restaurant, and hotel industries. The growth of food and hospitality industries demand qualified people to accommodate these needs of customers. Cookery has specialized subjects that are patterned to the standard requirement and the competency-based assessment of TESDA so that whoever wishes to work after SHS can be employed in the food and hospitality industry.
